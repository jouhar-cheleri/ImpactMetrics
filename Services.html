Impact Assessment

At Impact Metrics,we believe in the power of impact assessment, a crucial process that measures the effects of projects on society, ethics, economics, and politics. Impact assessment is not just a requirement by governments; it's a reflection of our shared humanity and commitment to positive change. We see impact assessment as more than a regulatory obligation. It's an expression of our shared responsibility to make the world a better place. By understanding and addressing the dimensions of our projects, we embrace our humanity while fulfilling government mandates. Together, we can create a brighter and more inclusive future for all.

Social Audit Services

A social audit is an independent evaluation of an organization's performance in achieving its social goals, making it an essential instrument of social accountability. In simpler terms, it involves a thorough analysis of a public utility's societal relevance. The concept of corporate social responsibility has evolved significantly in recent decades. An organization's societal impact and environmental responsibility are now integral to measuring its overall performance and sustainability. We offer comprehensive social audit services tailored to our clients' specific needs.

Participatory Appraisal (PA)

Participatory Appraisal (PA) is an approach rooted in learning and planning, leveraging local assets such as skills, land, agencies, communities, and networks. It empowers local communities to enhance their own environments using available resources. Our team offers a wide range of PRA/RRA (Participatory Rural Appraisal/Rapid Rural Appraisal) services, customized to meet our clients' specific requirements.

R&D for Monitoring & Evaluation Products

We provide research and development support for Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) products, including MIS (Management Information System) and GIS (Geographic Information System) development

Needs Assessments

A needs assessment is a crucial process for identifying the gaps between current and desired outcomes. It is an integral part of strategic planning, enabling organizations to outline the path towards achieving their goals. Our comprehensive need assessment services are tailored to align with our clients' requirements. including MIS (Management Information System) and GIS (Geographic Information System) development

Data Analysis and Reporting

Our end-to-end data research and analytics services empower clients to make well-informed decisions based on reliable data. With a team of data experts, advanced analytics tools, and a proven methodology, we assist enterprises and researchers in achieving their goals through accurate data analysis and reporting.

Training and Workshops for Capacity Building

Our training and workshop offerings cover a range of topics, including capacity building for social audits, various tools and frameworks for M&E, PRA tools and methods, impact assessments, and needs assessments. We customize these programs based on the specific needs of our clients.

Sustainability Consultancy

Sustainability consultants play a vital role in helping businesses incorporate responsible practices into their operations, especially in the face of climate crises. We provide support for strategic system analysis, designing sustainable system frameworks, and other related services, all of which are essential for businesses striving to achieve their sustainability objectives.
