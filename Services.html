Impact Assessment

Our Impact Assessment services gauge the real-world effects of your initiatives. We measure the change your projects bring to people's lives and the community. Our assessments provide actionable insights that guide you in making informed decisions, proving the success of your projects, and highlighting areas for improvement. Impact assessments are a vital tool to align your endeavors with your social responsibility goals.

Social Audit Services

Our Social Audit Services provide a comprehensive evaluation of your social initiatives. We conduct an in-depth analysis of the impact your projects have on communities and society at large. By scrutinizing processes, procedures, and outcomes, we ensure your resources are channeled effectively. Our audits go beyond compliance, focusing on the human aspect, resulting in valuable insights to optimize your efforts and enhance your public image.

Participatory Appraisal (PA)

Our Participatory Appraisals involve communities in the decision-making process. We work closely with local stakeholders to understand their needs, aspirations, and challenges. By incorporating their perspectives, we ensure that projects are tailored to the specific context and that they genuinely address community concerns. This participatory approach fosters a sense of ownership and results in more sustainable, community-driven solutions.

R&D for Monitoring & Evaluation Products

Our Research and Development activities focus on creating effective Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) products. We design tools and systems to track project progress, measure impact, and provide real-time insights. By continuously refining our M&E products, we help organizations adapt to changing needs, make informed decisions, and maximize the value of their initiatives.

Baseline Survey / Need Assessments

Baseline Surveys and Need Assessments form the foundation of impactful projects. We conduct in-depth studies to understand the current scenario and the requirements of the communities you aim to serve. These assessments guide the development of strategic plans, ensuring your resources are allocated efficiently to address the most pressing needs. By starting with a strong foundation, you set your initiatives up for success.

Data Analysis and Reporting

Our end-to-end data research and analytics services empower clients to make well-informed decisions based on reliable data. With a team of data experts, advanced analytics tools, and a proven methodology, we assist enterprises and researchers in achieving their goals through accurate data analysis and reporting.

Training and Workshops for Capacity Building

Our training and workshop offerings cover a range of topics, including capacity building for social audits, various tools and frameworks for M&E, PRA tools and methods, impact assessments, and needs assessments. We customize these programs based on the specific needs of our clients.

Sustainability Consultancy

Our Sustainability Consultancy services are your compass for a sustainable future. We provide expert guidance on integrating sustainable practices into your organization's core operations. Our consultants work closely with you to align your business strategies with environmental and social responsibility. By fostering sustainable practices, we help you reduce costs, mitigate risks, and enhance your reputation while contributing to a greener and more equitable world. Choose our consultancy to navigate the path to long-term sustainability and growth.
