About Us

At Impact Metrics LLP, our journey began with a vision of creating a profound societal transformation. Founded by two dedicated individuals who served as District Resource Persons in the Social Auditing Department of the Government of India, our story is one of transition from public service to a higher calling. Fueled by a passion for effecting lasting change, we embarked on this remarkable journey.

Joined by like-minded individuals, including retired bureaucrats and a formidable technical team, Impact Metrics LLP was born. Together, we combine decades of experience with fresh perspectives, marrying tradition with innovation

Our key differentiator lies in our embrace of technology and digital-driven methods for assessment, analysis, and reporting. While we value quantitative data, we're unyielding in our commitment to capture the qualitative essence of projects. We believe in understanding the human impact, the stories behind the numbers.

If you share any of our vision, partner with us on this inspiring path of creating positive change in society. Through our assessments, we not only help enhance your Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives and other social projects but also bolster your public image. Join hands with Impact Metrics LLP, where meaningful impact meets innovation, and together, we'll script a brighter future for all.
