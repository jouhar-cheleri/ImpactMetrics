Data Collection and Preparation

  • Gather all relevant information about the activities or social initiatives.
  • Compile data from government, corporate, or company records for the social audit.

Stakeholder Engagement and Feedback

  • Develop a systematic dialogue plan with stakeholders.
  • Collect valuable feedback from stakeholders using methods like questionnaires and discussions.

Social Performance Indicators

  • Create social performance indicators based on the gathered data.
  • Assess the effectiveness of social initiatives using these indicators.

Transparency and Expert Consultation:

  • Involve stakeholders as active participants in the assessment process.
  • Maintain transparency while protecting confidentiality when necessary.
  • Consult with experts and consider their opinions and suggestions when preparing the social audit framework
